Transparent by Architecture.

We've built a 'Trust Loop' that ensures your donation is never 'lost' in administrative overhead. Here's exactly how we track your impact.

1. Multi-Stage NGO Vetting

Before an NGO can list a single project, they undergo a rigorous 4-step verification process. We audit their financial history, valid registration, and physical presence at the project sites. Only the top 5% of applicants make it onto our platform.

  • Official Registration Check
  • On-ground Site Visitation

2. Escrow-locked Funds

When you donate, your money doesn't go straight to the NGO's general bank account. It's held in a project-specific escrow account. Funds are released incrementally as project milestones are met and verified by our system.

Protection: No fund diversion possible.

3. Real-Time Impact Proof

Once a milestone is reached, the NGO MUST upload geotagged photos, time-stamped receipts, and impact reports. You receive a notification instantly to see exactly where your contribution translated into change.
