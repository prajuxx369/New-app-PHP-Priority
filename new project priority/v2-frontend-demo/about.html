Born from a Trust Crisis.

In 2024, our founders realized a painful truth: billions are donated annually, but 40% of donors don't know where their money really goes.

We didn't just want to build another donation site. We wanted to build a transparency engine. A platform where 'Impact' isn't just a buzzword, but a verified fact backed by data, receipts, and photographic evidence.

The Transparency Charter

Zero-Hiding Policy

Every NGO on our platform agrees to financial audits per project.

Proof-of-Impact

Funds stay locked until visual or documentary proof is uploaded.

Community Vetting

Donors can flag updates that lack clarity or authenticity.
